🔬 Inside a Session

What Happens During Therapy?

Every session is a purposeful adventure. Explore what a typical therapy session looks like for each of our programs.

🖐️
Occupational Therapy
Hands-on learning through guided play

Building Skills for Everyday Life

OT sessions are designed to be fun and functional. Using play-based activities, our therapists help children develop the skills they need to participate fully in school, home, and community life.

1
Warm-Up ActivitySensory play to help the child transition into the session comfortably.
2
Goal-Targeted ExercisesFine motor, self-care, or sensory integration activities tailored to the child's plan.
3
Parent CoachingTherapist shares home strategies and progress updates with the family.
Sensory PlayFine MotorSelf-Care Tasks45 to 60 min per session
💬
Speech-Language Therapy
Finding every child's unique voice

Unlocking Communication Potential

Speech sessions are engaging and interactive. Our pathologists use games, stories, and structured exercises to help children express themselves, understand language, and connect with others.

1
Vocal Warm-UpBreathing and articulation exercises to prepare the child for focused work.
2
Language ActivitiesStory-based, game-driven exercises targeting the child's specific goals.
3
Real-World PracticeRole-play and social scripts to reinforce communication in everyday life.
ArticulationLanguage BuildingAAC Devices45 to 60 min per session
🧠
Behavioral Therapy
Building positive patterns for life

Shaping Positive Behaviors and Emotions

Behavioral therapy sessions provide a structured, supportive environment. Our therapists use evidence-based strategies to reduce challenging behaviors, build emotional regulation, and develop meaningful social skills.

1
Behavior Check-InBrief review of the child's mood and behavior patterns since the last session.
2
Skill-Building ActivitiesStructured exercises targeting emotional regulation, social cues, and positive behaviors.
3
Reinforcement and Family PlanTherapist guides family on how to reinforce skills and manage behaviors at home.
ABA MethodsSocial SkillsEmotional Regulation45 to 60 min per session
